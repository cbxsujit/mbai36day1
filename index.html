üë• Team Organization

CEO = Chief Energy Officer

Your job is to absorb all fear and exude hope. Your team looks to you for energy and direction.

‚ö†Ô∏è Don't Become the "Chaudhary" (Village Head)

‚ùå The Chaudhary Trap

  • ‚Ä¢ Everyone comes to YOU for every decision
  • ‚Ä¢ "Sanjay ne kiya", "Nitin ne kiya", "Sujit ne kiya" ‚Äî all complaints come to you
  • ‚Ä¢ Team waits for you, doesn't take ownership
  • ‚Ä¢ Every problem becomes a bottleneck
  • ‚Ä¢ Result: Stagnation, Stress, No Teamwork

‚úì The Coach Approach

  • ‚Ä¢ "You are capable, solve this yourself"
  • ‚Ä¢ "Nitin, you solve this. You have more experience than me"
  • ‚Ä¢ "If you have difficulty, take advice from Sahir"
  • ‚Ä¢ Force team to collaborate with each other
  • ‚Ä¢ Result: Growth & Freedom for you

"Step back, let them step up. Build a self-sufficient team that creates a self-sufficient business."
